Berserk, a Masterpiece

Berserk is a manga created by Mangaka Kentaro Miura and published for the first time in the Monthly Animal House Magazine from 1989 till 1992 and in the Young Animal Magazine from 1992
till present day. Set in a Midieval-Europe inspired Dark Fantasy world, the story follows Guts, a powerful and violent warrior in his quest for vengence again the Five God Hands, a group of
Demon/Gods, who are the main antagonists of the manga.

Berserk has over 40 million copies in circulation and features 40 volumes.



Plot

Berserk tells the story of Guts, a mercenary who evolves in the violent world of Midland, a Kingdom engaged in a Hundred Year War against the Tudor Empire. Mercenary since his
childhood, Guts catches the attention of Griffith, the charismatic leader of a mercenary group named "The Band of the Hawk". Griffith will forces Guts into joining him upon defeating
him in battle. Thanks to his strength, frearless attitude and battle aptitudes, Guts quickly rises through the ranks of the Hawks and becomes the renowned raid leader and Griffith's deadliest
subordinate. In the Band of the Hawk he will, for the first time in his violent life, meet people that he will consider as a family. Judeau, Pippin, Rickert and Casca, the Hawks only female
member.

The Band of the Hawk will join the force of the Kingdom of Midland and, victories after victories, become the most famous army of the war. During this time Guts will learn of Griffith
mysterious pendant, the Crimson Behelit, along with Griffith's dream to rule a Kingdom of his own.

Theme

Berserk explores a wide range of theme and topics. Free will, Destiny, Human nature, Morality... are few examples of the complexity and richness of the manga. In the manga, the characters
always follow their will, their emotions. There is no manicheism. There is no right or wrong in Berserk, only Free will, emotion and violence.

Friendship, Human relations and Love are also explored themes in Berserk. Guts lived a traumatic experience with the mercenary group that raised him. Since this, he has lost trust in people.
But in The Band of the Hawk he has found bonds, friendship, animosities and co-dependencies, maturing as an individual. In parallel, the other characters will also evolve through their
interactions with Guts. Casca for example, Griffith's first lieutenant, will discover that she also has a Dream.

The Religion is also another main theme in the series. Through multiple characters, Berserk speaks about religious fanatism, intolerance and the antagonism between Pagan Religions and
Monotheistic Religion.
